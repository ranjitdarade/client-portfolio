KALYANI KADAM

Makeup Artist · Educator · Entrepreneur

Lovingly called the "Queen of Makeup" across the industry and by her fans, Kalyani Kadam has come a long way from being a lawyer to one of the most prominent makeup artists in India. Today, she has millions of growing followers across Instagram, Facebook and YouTube.

Many years before her parents could give her permission, Kalyani would apply the hottest and the coolest shades of lip color to her lips and nail paint to her tiny nails. " I would sneak in to my much older cousins' home next door and go bonanza on the lipsticks and compact powders! ". Every part of Kalyani's life was always connected with makeup and artistry.

Coming straight to the present, Kalyani is a lawyer by education (stopped practicing in 2005) and instead decided to pursue her interest and passion. She opened her own Makeup studios in Gurgaon. She now runs a full fledged Makeup Academy as well. She has served thousands of brides till date.

Kalyani excels in all kinds of looks and makeup but her primary passion remains Bridal and Occasion makeup. Her brides display a class of their own and are instantly recognizable as "Kalyani Kadam Brides". She believes in bringing out the inherent beauty in you rather than create a fake plastic look.

Although Kalyani has come a long way from initial passion, the original driving force that led her into the beauty industry still remains as it was.

Kalyani still finds the most satisfaction when a client feels beautiful. "After I finish, I love the look on their face when they look in the mirror and see the results. No prizes for guessing that they actually feel beautiful. "
